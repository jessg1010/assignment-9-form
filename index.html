How well do you know the TV show "Gilmore Girls"?

"Gilmore Girls" is an American TV show that centers around a single mother named Lorelai (played by Lauren Graham) and the close relationship she has with her daughter (played by Alexis Bledel), who has the same first name but goes by Rory. The comedy show is known for its abundant pop-culture references and fast-paced dialogue. Although the show ended in 2007, a four-part revival is set to air on Netflix later this year.
